50th Caesars Anniversary

#Branding #Logo #Publication


Caesars Palace, Las Vegas, needed a logo to launch their 50th anniversary celebration for 2016. Incorporating the iconic Caesars Laural with the number “5” clearly defines the brand identity while portraying the “50th” in the anniversary logo.

Gordon Ramsey Fish & Chips

#Branding #Logo #Print #Publication


Gordon Ramsay wanted the highest quality creative for the launch of Gordon Ramsay Fish & Chips at The LINQ Promenade. To convey the themes and flavor of his new restaurant, we used the UK flag, fish and chips paper texture background and bold copy to match the boldness of Gordon Ramsay Fish & Chips.

Hobak Korean BBQ

Hobak Korean BBQ is famous for its quality dining, great service and incredible ambience that originated from South Korea.
